Research Statement

My name is Shreyas Skandan and I'm a masters student in the robotics programme at the University of Pennsylvania. I also work as a G.R.A.S.P Research Assistant at the Vijay Kumar Lab as part of the Precision Agriculture research team as well as at the Rehabilitation Robotics Lab. My research is in Deep Learning and Machine Perception. I'm currently exploring the applications of deep learning with visual data as well as applying stereoscopy to effeciently solve tracking problems.
